§ 04 The approach

Four
commitments.

What we promise on every engagement, written down so you can hold us to it.

I.

No juniors on your project.

Every line of code is written or reviewed by a senior engineer. AI helps us move faster — it doesn't replace judgment.

II.

Fixed scope, fixed price.

We commit upfront. If we underestimated, that's our problem, not yours. No hourly billing disputes, no scope drift surprises.

III.

You own everything.

Code lives in your GitHub. Models run in your cloud. Data never leaves your environment. When we leave, you're not stuck.

IV.

Boring tech where boring works.

We use Postgres before vector databases, cron jobs before agent frameworks. Ship the simple version, then iterate.
